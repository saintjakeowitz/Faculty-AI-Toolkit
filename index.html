Important disclaimer about AI tools

This toolkit is provided for informational and exploratory purposes only. It is not intended to promote or endorse the use of artificial intelligence tools in teaching, research, or administrative work.

Faculty should evaluate AI tools carefully, considering disciplinary norms, institutional policies, privacy requirements, and ethical responsibilities.

  • Student privacy and FERPA compliance
  • Academic integrity and authorship transparency
  • Bias, accuracy, and reliability of generated content
  • Copyright and intellectual property
  • Institutional or departmental AI policies
