AI in Teaching & Work (FERPA / integrity / transparency)
▾
A faculty-focused checklist (adapt to your institution and course policy).
  • FERPA: don't paste identifiable student data into external tools (names, IDs, grades, emails).
  • Grading: if using AI-assisted feedback, keep the instructor responsible for final evaluation and fairness.
  • Transparency: clarify what kinds of AI use are allowed in your course and what disclosure is required.
  • Bias: double-check language in feedback and rubrics; watch for inequitable assumptions.
  • Copyright/IP: verify tool terms for generated media; avoid mimicking branded styles or using unlicensed assets.
  • Accuracy: treat AI output as a draft; verify claims with sources (especially for lecture materials).
